Mobile App: Trail Talk - Hike Planner and Information Guide


"Likely" was drafted in anticipation of the easing of global travel restrictions - with tourism bouncing back to complete normalcy. The mobile app would give its users the ability to plan their trips from beginning to end, with all activities, sights, tours and transit options - as well as personalised recommendations - available at the user's convenience. Activities are bookmarked, reordered and reallocated in chunks, meaning that the user has complete control of their itinerary, and can adjust or readjust their plans to their heart's content, before making any confirmations.
